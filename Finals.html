About Our College

The UCLM College of Engineering is committed to providing world-class engineering education that combines theoretical knowledge with practical applications. Our programs are designed to prepare students for the challenges of the modern engineering landscape.

With state-of-the-art facilities and experienced faculty members, we foster innovation, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills among our students.

Engineering Courses Offered

Computer Engineering

Focuses on computer systems design, hardware-software integration, and embedded systems.

Electronics Engineering

Covers electronic circuits, digital systems, microprocessors, and communication systems.

Electrical Engineering

Focuses on power systems, control systems, and electrical machines.

Industrial Engineering

Optimizes complex systems and processes in manufacturing and service industries.

Mechanical Engineering

Deals with mechanical systems, thermodynamics, and manufacturing processes.
